Why this matters

Era is the textbook BG target: regulated infrastructure other agents connect TO, not an agent itself. The RIA wrapper is the legal authorisation layer for agent-initiated money movement, and Context is the MCP rails. Already shipped as the first personal-finance connector in Claude's directory — a lead position in the protocol layer for agentic personal finance.

Why you might pass

Crowded race — every personal-finance app and incumbent (Plaid, Intuit, the banks) is sprinting to ship MCP. Defensibility depends on the RIA moat holding and Era becoming the de-facto choice before bigger players cover the same surface. Mass-affluent segment is heavily contested by incumbents like Schwab and Fidelity-equivalents.
