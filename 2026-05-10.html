Marloo — AI Operating Platform for Financial Advisers ($10M seed, Blackbird)

Blackbird Ventures-led seed of $10M ($12.7M total in under 12 months) for a London startup automating post-meeting workflows for financial advisers — notes, compliance docs, client comms. 650+ advisory firms across six countries already using the platform, with 40%+ MoM revenue growth at raise. Marloo is adviser-facing (B2B), not client-facing, but sits directly in the operational stack advisers use to serve clients. The growth rate and multi-country traction in under 12 months is unusually strong for a B2B compliance workflow tool — strong PMF in underserved workflow territory. Score: 71/100.

Performativ — AI-Native Wealth Management OS (€11.9M/$14M Series A, Deutsche Börse)

Copenhagen-based Performativ raised a $14M Series A led by Deutsche Börse Group (with Rabo Investments/Rabobank) for an AI-native operating system for small and mid-sized European wealth managers. Founded 2020, the platform consolidates portfolio management, compliance, multi-custodian data, and client reporting. Series A stage means the BG direct entry window has passed, but the Deutsche Börse lead is a meaningful market signal: the largest continental European exchange operator is backing AI-native wealth infrastructure as a platform category. S-FRONT — Advisor Co-pilot Reinforces Spain

Flanks — AI Financial Advisor for Wealth Managers (product launch, Barcelona)

Barcelona-based Flanks launched a multi-agent AI Financial Advisor in March 2026, built on top of their existing infrastructure: 600+ custodian connections, 500K portfolios/month, regulated by the Bank of Spain. The AI advisor lets wealth managers query complex family structures and multi-custodian portfolios in plain language. January 2026 saw Flanks also launch EDX (European Data Exchange) with finReg360 — Europe's first standardised protocol for custodian-to-adviser financial data. No new funding (last round was Feb 2025 Series A). Key for Iberia coverage — Flanks is the most credible Spanish signal in BG's pipeline to date. Score: 66/100.

Round Treasury — Agentic Finance Automation ($6M seed)

London fintech Round raised $6M seed (Alstin Capital, Backed VC, Love Ventures, Paul Forster angel) for an agentic finance ops platform — natural language workflow builder that runs treasury sweeps, payroll, and invoice chasing autonomously with human-in-the-loop alerts via Slack/WhatsApp. Serving fintechs including Cleo and PostHog. Weak gap fit against core BG thesis (enterprise treasury ops ≠ investment management) but the agentic execution architecture is notable — full autonomous execution on financial rails with minimal human checkpoints.
