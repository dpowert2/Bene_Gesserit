# Pillar

**URL:** https://pillar.finance
**Gap:** S2 — Portfolio Construction & Risk Agent (Commodity Hedge Book)
**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** San Francisco, CA (US)
**Score:** 72

## One-liner
AI agent that continuously constructs and manages hedge portfolios for commodity-exposed businesses — autonomously ingesting exposure data, sizing positions, and executing trades against live risk.

## FinServ Signal
Clients include commodity trading firms, metal recyclers, and logistics companies managing FX and freight exposure. Pillar executes live trades, monitors risk in real time, and adjusts positions autonomously — not a dashboard, a genuine execution agent. Backed by a16z and Dara Khosrowshahi (Uber CEO). $20M seed at launch signals strong institutional conviction.

## Team
Co-founders Harsha Ramesh (CEO) and Chinmay Deshpande (CTO). Investors: Andreessen Horowitz (lead), Crucible Capital, Gallery Ventures, and Dara Khosrowshahi (Uber CEO). Round size: $23M total raised.

## Thesis
Pillar demonstrates the S2 Portfolio Construction & Risk agent pattern at production scale — the agent ingests raw exposure data from ERP, contracts, and spreadsheets, builds the hedge portfolio, and executes trades autonomously. The market is commodity hedging for industrial businesses rather than investment portfolios, which limits direct fit for your strategy, but the underlying capability (live portfolio construction + autonomous execution + continuous risk monitoring) is exactly the pattern you need in the investment context. This is a proof-of-concept for the S2+S5 stack that could be replicated in the investment agent space. **Hard note:** the $20M seed is large; at $23M raised total, this may already be past the shaping stage. Worth monitoring rather than pursuing aggressively.
