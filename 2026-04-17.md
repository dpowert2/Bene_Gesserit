### 3. Nava emerges from stealth — multi-gap financial agent guardrails ($8.3M seed)
**Gap:** Cat 5 + Cat 7 + Cat 10 — Safety / Payment Rails / Dispute Resolution | **Type:** Reinforces
**Source:** [Nava raises $8.3M to keep AI financial agents from going off the rails — Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/04/14/nava-seed-funding-ai-financial-agents/)

Nava emerged from stealth with an $8.3M seed led by Polychain and Archetype (joined by FalconX, Hack VC, and Eigen Labs contributors) to pioneer programmable guardrails for autonomous financial agents. The architecture combines escrow, policy enforcement, and dispute resolution: an agent proposes a transaction, Nava verifies it against the user's defined policy, and posts the reasoning publicly on-chain for audit. This simultaneously hits three validated pipeline gaps — safety guardrails (Cat 5), payment rails with verification (Cat 7), and dispute resolution (Cat 10). Previously evaluated and scored 5★ in your pipeline. The public funding announcement confirms viability. Key diligence question: can the on-chain verification architecture work in TradFi without requiring blockchain infrastructure from the institution?
