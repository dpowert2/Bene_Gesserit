From stakeholder themes
to working proofs of concept.

The FFF Programme pairs a small cohort of startups with FCAT experts to build real solutions against priorities our stakeholders have set — concluding in a live demonstration to senior leadership in January. It is not an accelerator and not an incubator; it is a working programme, one theme to one proof of concept.

i · How we work
Hand-in-hand with FCAT

Each startup is paired with FCAT engineers, designers and domain experts for the duration — not coached from the sidelines, not pitched to. We build together.

ii · What we build
A genuine proof of concept

Each team delivers a runnable, demonstrable PoC targeted at the theme it was selected against. Evaluated on its merits, not its pitch.

iii · How it ends
Live demo, senior audience

The January demo puts six months of real work in front of the senior figures who set the themes. No slide deck carries the story — the PoC does.
