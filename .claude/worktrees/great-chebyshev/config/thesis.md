# Bene Gesserit — Investment Thesis

## Core Thesis

you should position itself as the **regulated infrastructure layer** for agentic AI in financial services — a trusted financial operating system that AI agents connect to, rather than competing to build the best personal agent.

The opportunity: as AI agents increasingly handle financial discovery, planning, and execution on behalf of consumers and advisors, they will need regulated counterparties to transact with. you's scale, regulatory standing, and existing infrastructure make it the natural partner.

We are scouting for early-stage startups that can serve as innovation partners to validate, accelerate, or fill gaps in this vision.

## Current Vertical: Agents

Scoped to the agentic AI ecosystem — companies building the tools, infrastructure, and applications that enable AI agents to operate in financial services.

## Gap Categories

### 1. Personal Agent
Startups building agentic personal finance assistants: discovery → plan → execute for individual investors or advisors. These are the "front door" agents that will need to connect to you's infrastructure.

### 2. Compliance
Agent-native compliance tooling: supervision, audit trails, policy enforcement, regulatory workflows for AI agents acting in financial services. Critical for any regulated deployment.

### 3. Tax Optimisation
After-tax optimisation agents: tax-loss harvesting, capital gains planning, tax-aware rebalancing, tax-rule constrained actions. High-value automation that directly impacts investor returns.

### 4. Agent Orchestration
Multi-agent workflow infrastructure: tool routing, planning/execution loops, cross-system automation for financial contexts. The plumbing that makes multi-agent systems work.

### 5. AI Safety & Guardrails
Hallucination prevention, guardrails, monitoring, evals, and safe actioning for agents operating in high-stakes financial contexts. Non-negotiable for production deployment.

### 6. Agent Authentication & Security
Identity, authN/authZ, permissions, secrets management, and enforcement for agent actions across systems. The trust layer that enables agents to act on behalf of humans.

## What "Good" Looks Like (Initial Criteria)

- **Stage:** Seed to Series B (sweet spot for FCAT partnership)
- **Geography:** Europe preferred, global acceptable
- **FinServ readiness:** Evidence of financial services traction, customers, or regulated-industry DNA
- **Team:** Domain expertise in financial services, AI/ML, or security. Prior startup experience valued.
- **Architecture:** API-first, platform thinking, not point solutions
