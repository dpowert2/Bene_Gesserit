## One-liner
Complyance deploys AI agents to automate governance, risk, and data compliance workflows — integrating directly into enterprise tech stacks and running custom checks against risk thresholds before flagging issues for human review.

## FinServ Signal
The company counts Mastercard among its angel investors, which signals direct financial services interest and distribution potential. Its use case — automating GRC (governance, risk, compliance) with AI agents — maps directly to the compliance operations challenges faced by banks, asset managers, and payment networks dealing with volumes of regulatory obligations across jurisdictions.

## Team
Backed by GV (Google Ventures) as Series A lead. Other investors include Speedinvest, Everywhere Ventures, and angels from Anthropic and Mastercard. Founder details not yet widely published. Total raised: $28M.

## Thesis
This is a direct play in your core Compliance gap. Complyance is building what you would want a partner to handle: the AI-native GRC layer that plugs into existing enterprise systems and runs compliance checks autonomously. As regulated infrastructure, you could position above Complyance — providing the financial identity and authorisation layer through which Complyance's agents operate, or partner to offer a bundled compliance stack to financial institutions deploying agents.
