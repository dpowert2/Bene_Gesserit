# Eragon

**URL:** https://eragon.ai
**Gap:** Agent Orchestration
**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** San Francisco, CA, USA
**Score:** 55

## One-liner
Eragon is building an agentic AI operating system that replaces enterprise software GUIs with a natural-language prompt interface, connecting data, tools, and channels to deploy autonomous agents across business workflows.

## FinServ Signal
Early customers include large enterprises and YC-backed startups; the natural-language interface to back-office tooling (ERP, CRM, analytics) is directly applicable to financial operations, compliance workflows, and treasury automation. No disclosed fintech-specific customers, but the orchestration layer for enterprise workflows is a clear adjacency to regulated finance teams adopting agentic AI.

## Team
Founded by Josh Sirota (formerly enterprise SaaS operator); backed by Long Journey Ventures, Soma Capital, and Axiom Partners at $100M post-money valuation on $12M seed. Early traction includes Corgi ($108M-funded) and a cohort of YC startups.

## Thesis
Eragon's bet that enterprise software will be replaced by agent-driven, prompt-based interfaces is the orchestration layer thesis in action. If agents increasingly execute finance, compliance, and treasury workflows through LLM interfaces rather than point solutions, this gives you a potential integration surface to position as the governed, regulated infrastructure those agents call when they need to move money, authenticate, or maintain audit trails.
