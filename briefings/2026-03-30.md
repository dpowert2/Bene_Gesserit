### 7. Open Standards Will Unlock Agentic AI's Next Breakthrough in Fintech
**Gap:** Agent Orchestration | **Type:** Reinforces
**Source:** [FinTech Weekly](https://www.fintechweekly.com/magazine/articles/open-standards-agentic-ai-fintech-interoperability)

FinTech Weekly argues that interoperability — not model capability — is the binding constraint on agentic AI in financial services, noting that every payment processor, lender, and bank speaks a different data dialect. The article highlights the Agentic AI Foundation (Block, Anthropic, OpenAI under the Linux Foundation) and MCP as the emerging open-standards layer, with Stripe, Square, and Shopify already building MCP connectors. The implication for your strategy is significant: the infrastructure layer that normalises identity, permissions, and data access across fragmented financial systems becomes the natural home for AI agent trust and compliance. Open standards accelerate that opportunity by creating demand for a neutral, regulated party to sit in the middle.
