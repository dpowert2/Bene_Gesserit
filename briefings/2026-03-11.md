## 1. Bretton AI Raises $75M Series B to Scale Agent-Native Compliance Platform
Bretton AI (formerly Greenlite AI) closed a $75M Series B led by Sapphire Ventures, with backing from YC, Greylock, and Thomson Reuters Ventures, bringing total funding to $75M+. The company's AI agents handle high-volume financial crime workflows — AML, fraud, and KYC — at banks regulated by the OCC, FDIC, and Federal Reserve, including Robinhood, Mercury, and Gusto. It has completed 1.2M+ investigations and helped customers eliminate over 195,000 hours of manual compliance work, with the combined market cap of its client base growing from $150B to $1T in one year.
