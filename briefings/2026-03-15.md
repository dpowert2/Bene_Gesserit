### 4. FINRA Issues Specific Guidance on AI Agent Hallucination Risk at Broker-Dealers
**Gap:** Regulation | **Type:** Reinforces
**Source:** [FINRA Warns Brokers of Gen AI Hallucination Risks](https://www.wealthmanagement.com/regulation-compliance/finra-cautions-broker-dealers-to-catch-hallucinations-when-using-gen-ai)

FINRA's 2026 Annual Regulatory Oversight Report dedicated a full standalone section to GenAI for the first time, with specific guidance on agentic AI agents that can "act beyond the user's actual or intended scope and authority." The regulator now explicitly recommends narrow permissions, audit trails, and human checkpoints before agent execution — language that maps directly to the infrastructure primitives your regulated OS needs to provide. FINRA also confirmed it holds AI-generated communications to the same standards as human-generated ones, raising the compliance bar for any firm deploying agents at the customer interface.
