### 5. IBM, Auth0, and Yubico Form Human-in-the-Loop Framework for High-Stakes AI Agent Actions
**Gap:** Agent Authentication & Security | **Type:** Reinforces
**Source:** [Yubico, Auth0 and IBM form new partnership to secure agentic applications](https://www.ibm.com/new/announcements/securing-agentic-ai-why-automation-still-needs-human-oversight)

Presented at RSAC 2026 on March 24, IBM, Auth0, and Yubico introduced a production-ready Human-in-the-Loop authorization framework: IBM's WatsonX agents propose actions, Auth0 triggers CIBA-standard approval requests, and YubiKey taps provide cryptographic proof that a specific human physically authorised the decision. The model targets large financial transfers, production deployments, and sensitive data access. This is the enterprise blueprint for "supervised autonomy" — agents that act freely on routine tasks but require non-repudiable human sign-off for regulated actions. For any institution deploying AI agents, this framework is becoming the de facto governance standard.
