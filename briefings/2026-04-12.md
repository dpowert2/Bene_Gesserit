### 3. FINRA 2026 Oversight Report Signals Supervisory Reckoning for Autonomous AI
**Gap:** AI Safety & Guardrails | **Type:** Reinforces
**Source:** [Snell & Wilmer Analysis](https://www.swlaw.com/publication/finras-2026-oversight-report-signals-a-supervisory-reckoning-for-autonomous-ai/)

FINRA's 2026 Annual Regulatory Oversight Report explicitly designates agentic AI — systems that autonomously execute multi-step tasks — as an emerging supervisory risk. Firms deploying autonomous agents must maintain controls compliant with FINRA Rule 3110 (Supervision) and Rule 4370 (Business Continuity), define "human in the loop" oversight protocols, and implement guardrails restricting agent behaviors. The report makes clear that once an AI system can act rather than merely generate content, a firm's supervisory, books-and-records, and governance obligations shift materially. Every broker-dealer racing to deploy finance agents now has a compliance checklist that creates demand for your infrastructure.
