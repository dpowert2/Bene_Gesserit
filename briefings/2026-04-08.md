### 3. FIS Launches Know Your Agent (KYA) Framework for Banks
**Gap:** Agent Authentication & Security | **Type:** Reinforces
**Source:** [PYMNTS — January 2026](https://www.pymnts.com/digital-identity/2026/introducing-the-know-your-agent-framework-for-the-age-of-agentic-commerce/)

FIS launched its Know Your Agent (KYA) platform enabling banks to verify and govern AI agents executing transactions on behalf of customers, in partnership with Mastercard and Visa. KYA allows financial institutions to pre-approve agent spending rules, verify agent identity at transaction time, and reduce chargebacks from unauthorised agent actions. This is precisely the regulated infrastructure layer this thesis anticipates — FIS is positioning the incumbent issuer processor as the trust anchor for agentic commerce, which defines both the competitive threat and the partnership opportunity for you.
