SimCorp Launches Agent Launchpad — First Real "App Store for Financial Agents" at Institutional Scale

SimCorp (Deutsche Börse subsidiary, $220B+ AUM client base) launched Agent Launchpad on April 22 — an app store for AI agents that investment managers can discover and deploy directly within their portfolio management platform (SimCorp One). Orbit, an investment research platform processing 70M+ documents annually across 150K+ companies, is the first ecosystem partner, embedding its intelligence as an agent inside SimCorp workflows. Guardrails, structured audit trails, and human-in-the-loop controls are built in. This is not a startup — but it is the strongest institutional validation to date that the "app store for financial agents" architecture is the correct market structure. The institutional investment management layer is converging toward a marketplace model. The critical implication: the startup opportunity is building the agents that populate these marketplaces, not replicating the marketplace infrastructure.
