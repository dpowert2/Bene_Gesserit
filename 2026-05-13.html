KYA + Payment Rails New Signal San Francisco, USA

Skyfire — KYA-native payment rails with the KYAPay open standard ($9.5M total seed)

Payment and identity layer for the AI agent economy — Know-Your-Agent (KYA) verification, multi-rail programmable payments (card + stablecoin + invoice), OAuth2/OIDC compatibility, and the KYAPay open standard for verified agent transactions. $9.5M total seed across two rounds with Neuberger Berman, Inception Capital, and Arrington Capital. Founded 2023 by Amir Sarhangi (ex-Google identity) and Craig DeWitt (payments). The KYAPay open-standard angle is the genuine differentiator — community-governed alternative to Visa TAP, Trulioo DPA, Sumsub, and ERC-8004 — echoing the APEX Standard signal in execution. Archetype-slot caveat: KYA is already covered by Vouched (5★, $17M Series A in March) and Payment Rails by Nava (5★) + Natural (4★). The cycle-15 archetype-displacement pattern (Quartr 4★ vs Edgehound) suggests Skyfire is likely to score below merit because the slots are filled. Watch how Vouched's KYA platform and Skyfire's KYAPay standard interact; one may become an interoperability layer for the other. Score: 67/100.
