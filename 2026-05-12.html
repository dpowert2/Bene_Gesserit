Reinforces S6 — Execution Agent Technical Artifact

APEX Standard — Open Protocol for Agentic Trading (v0.1.0-alpha, MCP-based)

The most significant technical signal in BG's history. APEX Standard is an open, MCP-based specification defining how AI agents communicate with brokers, exchanges, dealers, and other execution venues — "FIX for the agentic era." Created March 20, 2026; actively developed (last push May 9). Community-governed (CC-BY 4.0 / Apache 2.0), with 19 mandatory tools across five domains (session, account, orders, market data, risk), 167 conformance checks, and reference implementations in TypeScript, Rust, Go, and Java. Why this matters: APEX Standard's emergence is the S6 (Execution Agent) market's equivalent of FIX Protocol's 1992 launch — it signals practitioners believe the category is real enough to warrant a shared open standard before any single vendor locks it down. The startups that first build conformant implementations will be identifiable BG-stage targets. Watch for commercial entities that emerge from or align with this protocol.
