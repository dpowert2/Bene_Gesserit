## Thesis
Buy-side research workflows remain the bottleneck between signal and trade. Every day a PM spends on filings, earnings prep, 13F reads, and peer comp is a day not spent on conviction-building and client-facing work. Your thesis assumes this gap will be filled by specialist agents, not general-purpose LLMs. Kimpton is exactly that: a domain-trained research agent designed for the specific JTBD of a portfolio manager, with auditable sourcing (grounded computation, cited FactSet data) and output formatted as executable Trade Proposals. The product-market fit is narrow and well-defined. Platform risk is low because the core moat is fund-operator knowledge encoded in the agent, not just an LLM wrapper. Expresses the thesis directly: if specialist agents become the mode of investment work, Kimpton becomes the S1/S2 default for the buy side.
