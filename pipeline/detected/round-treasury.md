## One-liner
AI-native finance automation platform that lets finance teams describe workflows in plain English, then builds and runs them autonomously — covering treasury sweeps, payroll, invoice chasing, and expense management 24/7.

## FinServ Signal
Moderate: Not investment-focused, but the Agentic Workflow Builder and Autonomous Payroll module demonstrate mature agentic execution (not just chatbot interfaces) running on real financial rails. Customers include Cleo and PostHog. Backed by Alstin Capital (Berlin), Backed VC, Love Ventures, with Paul Forster (Indeed co-founder) as angel. Passion Capital (£46M Fund IV targeting AI + fintech) reinvested, which signals continued conviction.

## Team
Founded by two ex-finance operators. Alstin Capital-led seed with Backed VC and Love Ventures. Angel investment from Paul Forster (co-founder, Indeed). Previously backed by Passion Capital.

## Thesis
Round is operating in enterprise finance operations (treasury, payroll) rather than investment management — weaker BG gap fit than specialist agents. However, the agentic execution architecture is notable: the Workflow Builder creates autonomous workflows from natural language and runs them against real financial rails, including yield accounts and contractor payments, with Slack/WhatsApp notifications only when human input is needed. This is a template for how agentic finance automation will operate, and the pattern is relevant to higher-value workflows (portfolio rebalancing, cash deployment, structured product execution). Worth watching for whether Round expands into investment-adjacent use cases. Iberian geographic coverage not relevant here.
