# Panta

## One-liner
AI-native commercial insurance brokerage where the entire operations layer — submissions, follow-ups, COIs, endorsements — is run by autonomous agents with no human brokers.

## FinServ Signal
Licensed commercial insurance brokerage operating in the Excess & Surplus (E&S) market ($125B annual premium). In production since December 2025. Named clients include SpaceX suppliers, Marvel Entertainment, and The Weeknd. Agents operate inside carrier portals, email, phone, and PDFs — same tools as human brokers. YC W26 backing.

## Team
Vincent (CEO) — former Google AI/ML engineer, led recommendation models on Vertex AI and NotebookLM Enterprise. Frank (CTO) — former senior engineer at Apple, built Apple's Gen-AI chatbot and Rust infrastructure. Early-stage, small team.

## Thesis
Panta is a specialist agent operating in regulated insurance, not an infrastructure platform for other agents. The thesis fit is indirect: Panta demonstrates that autonomous AI agents can operate as licensed financial service providers (brokerage, fiduciary-equivalent functions) in regulated markets — which is a structural proof point for the BG app-store model. However, Panta itself is a vertically integrated company (agent + brokerage licence + carrier relationships), not an API or marketplace participant. Watch if it opens a developer/API layer or if a "Panta for other agent orchestrators" shape emerges. Not a direct BG pipeline match; scored accordingly.
