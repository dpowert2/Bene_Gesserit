## One-liner
AI-powered trading journal with automatic trade tagging based on technical and fundamental analysis. Personalised insights and pattern recognition surface what's working and what isn't across closed positions. First plausible candidate in the previously empty S10 cell — the consumer-side demonstration that AI-mediated post-trade learning has live retail-trader product-market fit.

## FinServ Signal
Bootstrapped indie SaaS for retail traders. Auto-tags trades on technical patterns (breakout, pullback, mean reversion) and fundamental context (earnings, sector, macro). Not regulated-finserv DNA. The product analyses thousands of trades in seconds and surfaces personalised insights — the consumer-side analogue of the institutional TCA / attribution layer that occupies the same S10 cell on the broker archetype.

This is a category-validation card, not a graduate-into-the-marketplace candidate. The B2B-broker layer (Markets Media, The Trade News surfaces) wasn't probed deeply for S10 in the cycle-14 seed pass; that path is on the cycle-15 backlog.

## Thesis
TraderSage is the demonstration card for the S10 archetype-A path — the same way QuantWheel demonstrates the S5 archetype-A path. AI-tagged trade journaling has live indie SaaS competitors (TradeLog Pro, JournalPlus, Edgewonk, TraderSync) and the category is mature enough to support multiple players. The novelty-gate read is mixed: the auto-tagging on technical + fundamental analysis is a real product feature, but the framing is consumer-marketing rather than category-reinvention. Track alongside TradeLog Pro / JournalPlus as category-validation; the institutional-side S10 candidate (post-trade TCA attribution for asset managers / brokers) is a separate scout that lives on a C-archetype surface (Markets Media, Risk.net) and remains backlog.
