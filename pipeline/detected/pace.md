# Pace

**URL:** https://withpace.com
**Gap:** Agent Orchestration
**Stage:** Series A
**Geo:** United States
**Score:** 78

## One-liner
Pace builds agentic AI systems that autonomously handle insurance operations — submissions intake, claims processing, and document-heavy back-office workflows — replacing the offshore BPO layer at regulated insurers.

## FinServ Signal
$10M Series A led by Sequoia Capital (partner Bryan Schreier). Production customers include Prudential Financial, The Mutual Group, and Newfront — all regulated insurance entities with stringent operational and compliance requirements. The target market is a $70B annual BPO market in insurance, expanding to ~$400B across all financial services operations. Selected by Prudential Financial for agentic AI deployment across its insurance operations — one of the strongest single-customer validation signals seen at this stage.

## Team
CEO: Cuffe — previously co-founder of Cheer, acquired by Retool (2020). Backed by Sequoia, Bryan Schreier.

## Thesis
Pace is occupying the BPO replacement layer inside regulated insurance — a position with exceptional defensibility because switching costs are high, compliance requirements are strict, and the human workflow knowledge required to automate these tasks is hard to replicate. Insurance is the adjacent market closest to traditional financial services, and Pace's Prudential relationship gives it direct finserv credibility. This gives you a potential infrastructure partner for any agent orchestration play in the insurance operations corridor — or a competitive signal if you're targeting similar back-office workflow automation in banking.
