## One-liner
Wealth intelligence platform that processes 500K+ portfolios monthly across 600+ institutional custodian connections, now launching an AI Financial Advisor — a multi-agent co-pilot that lets advisors interrogate complex multi-custodian client portfolios in plain language.

## FinServ Signal
High: Regulated as AISP by the Bank of Spain under the ECB; 100+ financial institutions including tier-1 banks and multi-family offices across Europe and MENA. In January 2026 Flanks launched the EDX (European Data Exchange) with finReg360 as Europe's first standardised protocol for financial data exchange between custodians, wealth managers, and advisers — this is serious financial infrastructure work, not demo-ware.

## Team
Barcelona-based founding team. €14M Series A raised February 2025. The EDX initiative and AI Financial Advisor launch signal a well-resourced product roadmap.

## Thesis
Flanks is building from the data layer up: they own the custodian connectivity (600+ connections, 33 countries) and are now surfacing AI advisory capabilities on top. This is a strong architecture — the AI advisor works because the data is complete and standardised, not patched together. Spain / Iberia is underrepresented in BG's current pipeline and Flanks is an important signal for the region. The product launch (March 2026) is a reinforcement of the S-FRONT thesis and confirms institutional adoption of multi-agent advisory tools in regulated European markets.
