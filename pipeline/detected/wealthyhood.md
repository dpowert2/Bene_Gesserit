## One-liner
An app-based investment platform for younger European investors — fractional shares, recurring investments, AI-powered portfolio suggestions — now adding B2B banking distribution.

## FinServ Signal
€6M Series A led by Bank of Cyprus — a significant strategic validator from a regulated European bank, suggesting the B2B distribution angle is credible. Total raised ~€9.6M. 60,000+ users in UK, 10,000+ in Greece after local launch. Adding B2B services to offer the platform to banks and financial institutions as white-label. AI investing features cited but product is primarily automated recurring investment rather than agentic.

## Team
Founded 2020 by Alexandros Christodoulakis and Konstantinos Faliagkas, both Greek. Operating from London with market presence in UK and Greece. The Bank of Cyprus anchor gives regional credibility in Southeast Europe.

## Thesis
Wealthyhood sits in R3 (Wealth Management Reinvention) — it's democratising access to investment markets for younger retail investors with lower friction than legacy brokers. The AI component is present but not deeply agentic; this is closer to a next-gen robo-advisor than an autonomous investment agent. The B2B pivot (selling to banks) is strategically sensible but shifts it further from the direct-to-investor agentic experience BG is tracking. Most interesting for BG as a Greek/Southeast European coverage signal — one of the few investment-focused startups from that region in the pipeline.
