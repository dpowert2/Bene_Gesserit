## One-liner
Blockchain-anchored escrow, policy enforcement, and dispute resolution infrastructure for autonomous AI financial agents — programmable guardrails that govern how agents interact with capital and other agents.

## FinServ Signal
Purpose-built for financial agent workflows: holds funds in escrow, verifies agent-proposed transactions against user-defined policies, releases or blocks on outcome verification, and logs every decision on-chain for audit. Investors include Polychain and Archetype (specialist crypto/fintech VCs) plus FalconX and Eigen Labs contributors. Explicitly targets "enterprises deploying agents that manage capital." Previously evaluated and voted on (5★, followup requested) in the BG pipeline.

## Team
Emerged from stealth April 14, 2026. $8.3M seed co-led by Polychain and Archetype. Additional investors: FalconX, Hack VC, Seed Club Ventures, Sreeram Kannan (Eigen Labs / EigenCloud). The blockchain-native investor base reflects the on-chain verification architecture.

## Thesis
Nava hits three validated gaps simultaneously: agent safety guardrails (Cat 5), payment rails with verification (Cat 7), and dispute resolution (Cat 10). The on-chain reasoning log is architecturally interesting — every accept/reject decision is publicly auditable, which could satisfy the "agent decision traceability" requirement for regulated deployments. The blockchain-native foundation introduces a question: can this operate in TradFi without on-chain infrastructure? That's the key diligence question. The multi-gap positioning and 5★ prior vote confirm this is high-priority.
