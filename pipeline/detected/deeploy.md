## One-liner
AI governance platform that makes ML deployments accountable — embeds explainable AI (XAI) at the centre of MLOps, with real-time monitoring, automatic audit trails, model-drift detection, and instant alerts for compliance risks. Open-source-framework-compatible and listed on AWS Marketplace.

## FinServ Signal
Direct relevance to the BG Audit & Explainability gap that has Alinia (Mouro/Santander seed) as the only first entry. Deeploy's positioning — explainability + audit trails + monitoring at the heart of MLOps — is exactly what regulated finserv buyers need to satisfy "why did the agent recommend this trade?" requirements. Not yet a finserv-specific play, but the architecture transfers cleanly. AWS Marketplace listing suggests enterprise sales motion.
