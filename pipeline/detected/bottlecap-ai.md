## FinServ Signal
Weak. First commercial product is "Pulse: Community News" on iOS — consumer news, not finserv. They're building general LLMs and an "app studio" to commercialise applications, not specialist agents in finserv.

## Team
Tier-A research credentials. Mikolov invented word2vec. Funded by 20VC plus founders of Canva, ElevenLabs, Datadog, HuggingFace, Lovable, Synthesia, Pigment.

## Thesis
This is a CHALLENGES-type signal, not a fit. The BG hard filter rejects "foundation model" startups in favour of specialist agents that USE foundation models for specific finserv outcomes. BottleCap is upstream of where BG plays. Worth tracking because:
1. CEE breakout signal — Czech, $7.5M seed at top-tier conference
2. Mikolov's involvement makes it a thesis-watch item — if their efficient LLM architecture lands, it changes the unit economics of every specialist agent BG would back.
