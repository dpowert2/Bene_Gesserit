# Astor

**URL:** https://www.astor.ai / https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/astor
**Gap:** S-Front (Personal Financial Agent)
**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** San Francisco, CA (US)
**Score:** 69

## One-liner
AI investment advisor that plugs into your brokerage account and delivers personalised, fiduciary-grade advice via text or voice for $15/month.

## FinServ Signal
Already live with ~4,000 paying subscribers at $15–$40/month. Founder Bruno Koba obtained a Series 65 advisor licence — advice is fact-checked by the firm's own compliance agents. Connects directly to investor brokerage accounts for real portfolio context. Regulated RIA structure from day one.

## Team
Bruno Koba (co-founder Nubank, Monashees) and Daniel Tulha (Stripe, Robinhood). Seed round of $5M led by Monashees with participation from Y Combinator (W26), Goodwater Capital, and executives from Stripe and OpenAI.

## Thesis
Astor sits squarely at the front door of your investment journey — the "S-Front" layer that every other specialist agent flows through. It democratises access to fiduciary advice (currently gated behind $500k+ minimums) and is already generating revenue with real users. The licensing structure and brokerage integration show regulatory seriousness rare at this stage. If Astor exposes an agent API, it becomes a natural orchestration layer for the specialist agents in the rest of the portfolio. Key risk: currently positioned as a consumer chatbot rather than an agent platform — watch for any MCP server or API exposure announcements.
