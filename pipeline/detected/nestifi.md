## Thesis
This is the rare clean fit for the two-axis test the generational thesis sets: a **European-founded builder pointed at the US Great-Wealth-Transfer market**. It sits across three of the new cells — the family graph (multi-generational picture), goal-funding (tracking family contributions), and the life-event/orchestration layer — without claiming to be the whole family-office super-app. The white-label-into-institutions model is exactly how an incumbent platform would harness it rather than compete with it. Watch for the consultancy-vs-product line and whether the "AI" is genuinely agent-callable or a retention dashboard.
