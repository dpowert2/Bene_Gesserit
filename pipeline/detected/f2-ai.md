## One-liner
YC-backed AI platform that delivers institutional-grade financial analysis across CIMs, data rooms, Excel, and PDFs for private credit and PE deal teams.

## FinServ Signal
Spun out of Arc (the fintech revenue-based finance company), purpose-built for private markets investors. 7x month-over-month growth in platform usage and 60x ARR growth since launch in September 2025. Launched "Audit Mode" bringing defensible, explainable AI outputs to private markets underwriting. Positioned as the Bloomberg Terminal for private credit — AI-native from day one. Attending Credit Invest USA 2026 as a sponsor.

## Team
Arc alumni team, Y Combinator-backed (YC W26). Investors include NFX, Left Lane Capital, Y Combinator, Alumni Ventures, Carya Venture Partners, Sarah Smith Fund.

## Thesis
The private credit and PE market runs on CIMs, data rooms, and LP reports — largely unstructured documents that traditional research tools can't parse. F2 ingests these at scale and delivers analysis with "near-perfect accuracy" and an audit trail. For you, the most interesting angle is the Audit Mode launch: if F2's intelligence layer becomes agent-callable, it could function as the S1 research signal provider for institutional credit decisions, complementing the equity-market-focused research agents already in the pipeline. The risk: F2 is currently optimised for institutional deal teams (PE funds, private credit managers), not retail-facing agents or consumer brokerages. Watch for an API/agent exposure path — that would move the score into the mid-80s.
