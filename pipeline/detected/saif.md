## One-liner
The trust layer for physical AI. Core product NEXUS is a real-time guardian for AI-driven systems that monitors actions and intervenes within pre-defined rules to prevent unsafe behaviour, ensuring embodied AI operates predictably and securely. Companion products: Forge (deployment tooling) and Prism (actionable safety insights powered by NEXUS).
