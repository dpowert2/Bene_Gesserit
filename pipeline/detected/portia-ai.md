# Portia AI

**URL:** https://portia.ai
**Gap:** Gap #2 — AI Safety & Guardrails (agent oversight & control)
**Stage:** Seed
**Geo:** London, UK
**Score:** 67

## One-liner
Open-source SDK and cloud platform that gives developers predictable, controllable, human-in-the-loop AI agents built specifically for production in regulated industries.

## FinServ Signal
Founded by Emma Burrows (former CTO of Stripe UK) and Mounir Mouawad (former product lead at Stripe). Explicitly targets regulated industries including financial services as a primary use case. Three core pillars — predictability, controllability, authentication — map directly onto the regulatory requirements of financial agent deployment. General Catalyst, one of the most active fintech VCs globally, led the seed round.

## Team
Co-founders from Stripe UK (Emma Burrows as CTO, Mounir Mouawad in product). Backed by General Catalyst (lead) and First Minute Capital. £4.4M seed raised April 2025. Open-source SDK on GitHub (composability signal — aligns with agent-callable API preference).

## Thesis
Portia solves the gap between "agent capability" and "agent control" — the exact problem regulators and compliance teams raise about deploying third-party agents in financial services. The open-source SDK lowers the barrier for finserv teams building agents that need deterministic, auditable behaviour. The Stripe founding team gives this meaningful credibility with the payments/finserv infrastructure community. General Catalyst's lead signals confidence in the regulated-deployment angle. Relative to incumbent options (LangChain, CrewAI), Portia's explicit focus on human-in-the-loop and authentication is differentiated and thesis-aligned. The horizontal positioning is a slight drag — this serves any regulated industry, not just financial services — but the fintech-first pedigree of the team and the finserv use-case emphasis reduce the adjacency concern.
