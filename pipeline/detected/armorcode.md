## One-liner
ArmorCode is a unified exposure management platform that uses agentic AI to unify, prioritise, and remediate risks across applications, cloud, infrastructure, and AI systems at enterprise scale.

## FinServ Signal
Nearly 80% of its Fortune 500 and Fortune 1000 customers are pushing for expanded Agentic AI capabilities, many of which are financial institutions. Phil Venables — former Goldman Sachs CISO and Google Cloud CISO — joined the board in March 2026, signalling deep regulated-industry relevance. The platform processes over 200 billion findings per year, a scale consistent with large financial services environments.

## Team
Nikhil Gupta (CEO) and team. Investors include Cheyenne Ventures, Ballistic Ventures, Highland Capital, Sierra Ventures, NGP Capital, Harmonic Growth Partners, Tau Ventures, and Cervin Ventures. Board: Phil Venables (former Goldman Sachs/Google Cloud CISO).

## Thesis
As you position as the regulated infrastructure layer for agentic AI in financial services, ArmorCode represents a potential partner for AI governance and exposure management tooling. Financial institutions deploying agents need a control plane that unifies risk visibility across AI, application, and cloud surface — ArmorCode provides exactly that. Its Goldman Sachs CISO pedigree on the board gives it credibility in regulated enterprise and creates a natural commercial bridge into large bank and insurer AI programmes.
