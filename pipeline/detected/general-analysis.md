## Thesis
As your agentic brokerage platform onboards third-party specialist agents, the attack surface grows: adversarial prompts, hallucinated trade logic, agent impersonation. General Analysis provides the testing and defensive layer — adversarial simulation, guardrails, agent identity management, observability — that makes it safe to run third-party agents in production. The gap reopened when Overmind (previously the 5★ AI Safety candidate) declined participation. General Analysis addresses the same architectural need: a security-intelligence layer between the orchestration platform and the third-party agents. The "finance customers in production" signal is the clearest differentiation from pure AI-lab safety research. Key open question: does the product explicitly govern *third-party/external* agents or only protect an institution's own agents from external attack?
