# Era

## One-liner
AI-powered registered investment adviser delivering fiduciary investment management through third-party AI agents — no human advisors, fully regulated.

## FinServ Signal
SEC-registered RIA (Era Financial Advisers LLC) with fiduciary authority to act on users' behalf: moving money, building emergency savings, investing funds, and rebalancing portfolios autonomously. 15,000+ consumer app users. Platform integrates with Anthropic Claude, OpenAI ChatGPT, and Google Gemini as the agent execution layer. In beta, zero AUM disclosed in initial Form ADV — early but structurally regulated.

## Team
Co-founded by Alex Norcliffe (former head of design at Stripe) and Lindsay Brady (former Stripe head of operations, now COO). Five full-time employees. Backed by MaC Venture Capital, Third Kind Venture Capital, Clocktower Ventures, K5 Ventures, Northzone, Protagonist, and strategic angels from Stripe, Netflix, Google, and Plaid.

## Thesis
This gives you the clearest current example of the "regulated retail front-door" archetype — an AI agent platform with SEC registration and fiduciary authority that already routes execution through third-party AI systems (Claude, GPT, Gemini). The structural model is exactly the S-Front sub-track: consumer-facing, RIA-registered, using specialist agents for advice and execution. If Era exposes its portfolio management and planning capabilities as an agent-callable API, it becomes a platform layer for the agent marketplace. Worth a conversation about the API/integration story.
