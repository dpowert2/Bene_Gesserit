## One-liner
AI operating platform for financial advisers that automates note-taking, compliance documentation, and client communication, positioning itself as the core OS for advisory firms.

## FinServ Signal
Strong: 650+ advisory firms across six countries already on the platform, with revenue growing over 40% month-on-month at time of raise. Marloo is specifically built for FCA-regulated advisory practices — compliance auditability is a core feature, not an afterthought. The $10M seed brings total capital to $12.7M in under twelve months, with Blackbird Ventures leading both the pre-seed and seed rounds.

## Team
Founded in 2025. Team details not disclosed at launch. Blackbird Ventures led both rounds, which carries significant conviction signal — Blackbird is the pre-eminent Australian/UK deep-tech seed fund.

## Thesis
Marloo is capturing the advisor layer of the personal wealth stack. Where S-FRONT is the client-facing agent that understands the customer's goals, Marloo is the advisor-facing OS that handles everything the adviser used to do manually after a client meeting. These are complementary — a complete S-FRONT architecture likely needs both. The 40%+ MoM growth rate and 650-firm adoption in under twelve months indicates strong product-market fit in an underserved workflow category. The UK/Australia/US expansion roadmap is well-sequenced for regulated markets. Watch for whether Marloo layers in client-facing capabilities (moving from advisor tool to advisor + client agent) — that would be the moment it becomes a direct S-FRONT player.
