# The Balanced Investor Club

**URL:** https://thebalancedinvestorclub.com/
**Gap:** S9 — Behavioural Coaching Agent
**Stage:** Pre-Seed / Launch
**Geo:** US
**Score:** 62

## One-liner
AI-native behavioural finance companion helping everyday investors understand their own decision-making patterns and invest with greater discipline and calm.

## FinServ Signal
The Morningstar "Mind the Gap" dataset underpins the product thesis: the average dollar invested in US mutual funds and ETFs earned 1.2 percentage points per year less than the funds themselves returned over 10 years — ~15% of aggregate returns lost to mistimed investor decisions. The product covers 13,000+ US stocks, ETFs, and crypto instruments. Expansion into FX planned for late 2026. Importantly: explicitly educational only, no investment recommendations or trade execution.

## Team
No prominent founder background surfaced in initial research. Founded February 2026; just completed commercial launch. Very early stage.

## Thesis
This is one of the first clean fits for the S9 (Behavioural Coaching Agent) gap — a category that has had zero pipeline companies to date. The product addresses Stage 13 of the investment process: the emotional layer that destroys returns. The problem is real and quantified (the Morningstar data is compelling). The main limitation is the "educational only" positioning — the product currently lacks agent-callable API functionality. For the thesis to fully apply, you'd want this exposed as a callable service that other agents in the marketplace can invoke when a user's behaviour signals panic-selling risk. But as a first entry in an empty gap, it's worth tracking.
