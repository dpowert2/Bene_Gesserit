## One-liner
An API-first RegTech platform automating e-invoicing and real-time tax reporting across multi-jurisdictional compliance environments — now integrating AI models.

## FinServ Signal
Founded 2018. 70M+ invoices processed since 2019, 450+ customers, 10+ countries, 120,000 connected entities. Led by P101 SGR (Italy's leading B2B tech VC) with Sella Direct Ventures (Banca Sella's fintech investment arm). The VIDA directive (VAT in the Digital Age) and continuous transaction control mandates across EU are structural tailwinds — A-Cube is pre-positioned at the compliance infrastructure layer. AI integration is on the roadmap rather than core today.

## Team
Long-standing team (founded 2018), deep Italian tax law and e-invoicing expertise. Limited public profile but 8 years of operational scale is a strong signal of product-market fit.

## Thesis
A-Cube is B2B tax compliance infrastructure rather than a direct-to-investor tax optimisation agent (S4). The gap fit is weak against BG's thesis — tax optimisation for investors (harvesting, wash-sale, multi-jurisdiction) is a different product from e-invoicing compliance for businesses. That said, Italy is one of the most under-represented geographies in the BG pipeline, P101 is a credible lead, and the VIDA directive is a genuinely large structural catalyst. Logged as a reference signal for the Italian RegTech/tax category. If A-Cube expands into investment-adjacent tax reporting (CGT, dividend withholding, cross-border portfolio compliance), it could become a stronger fit.
