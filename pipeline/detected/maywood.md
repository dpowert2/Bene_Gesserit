## Thesis
Trade structuring — the analytical work between idea and executed deal — is one of the most document-intensive, manually burdened workflows in finance. A mid-market M&A deal generates hundreds of pages of CIMs, diligence trackers, buyer analyses, and model iterations. Maywood compresses weeks of associate work into a living deal workspace. Your thesis places S3 as a high-priority specialist gap (status: empty, specialist_bonus: +15). Maywood fills it with a product that is deal-flow native rather than research-repurposed. The IB angle means financial services is not just a use case — it is the only use case. Risk: S3 gap map covers asset-location + tax-aware advisor copilot positioning; Maywood is sell-side M&A. Partial gap fit but the closest S3 signal found in the scan and warrants monitoring.
