## One-liner
Tax-focused AI-native RIA launched October 2025 by Bill Harris (former CEO of PayPal, former CEO of Intuit, founder of Personal Capital — sold to Empower for $1B). Self-funded with $10M+ of his own capital. Launches with $100M AUM and 50 employees, half on technology, half on advisory and ops. Mass-customises portfolios for affluent and high-net-worth clients using a not-too-hot, not-too-cold blend of call-centre humans and AI. Uses Alphathena as its underlying direct-indexing and TLH engine.

## FinServ Signal
The most credentialed founder in the S4 + R3 cells. Bill Harris's track record: built Personal Capital from 2009 (sold to Empower for $1B), CEO at PayPal during the Elon Musk-era launch chaos, CEO at Intuit. The five-year rest, two-year ramp-up, and explicit choice to self-fund rather than raise institutional capital ("saves a lot of time") signals confident-operator energy. The tax-focused mandate is precise — this isn't a "wealth platform" with tax as a feature; tax-aware investing is the lead.

Three offices at launch (Miami / Dallas / Raleigh NC) with planned expansions to New York and Palo Alto suggests an ambitious go-to-market — not a sandbox experiment. The Alphathena partnership is a buy-don't-build signal that Harris reads the market for direct-indexing infrastructure as already mature enough to source rather than recreate.

## Thesis
Evergreen Wealth is the canonical archetype reference for "AI-native RIA replacing the human-advisor stack" — alongside Astor, Era, Autonomous, and the Range archetype Dave already carded. For S4 the relevance is sharp: Evergreen is *explicitly* tax-focused, uses agent-driven portfolio management, and has chosen Alphathena (an already-strong S4 candidate in this same cycle) as the underlying engine. This is the cleanest live B-archetype demonstration of the Studio "Intelligent Tax Agent" thesis with a real RIA buyer integrated.

The stage signal is the meaningful caveat — this is **not** a seed/Series-A startup. It's a self-funded mature-founder venture that launched at $100M AUM scale. The same gate that flagged Range S3 applies here: past the seed-window edge, scoring soft-discounted but not auto-rejected because the company is *new* (October 2025) even if the founder is established. Track as an archetype reference that informs scoring of future S4 candidates rather than as a primary pipeline target.

The novelty-gate read: not a "operating system for advisors" framing — Evergreen is *itself* a regulated RIA with AI underneath. That's structurally different from the Marloo/Asseta-AI/Avantos 1★ shape and aligns more with the Personal Capital / Empower / Range trajectory.
