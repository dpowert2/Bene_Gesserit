## One-liner
A secure platform that lets someone holding power of attorney manage, store and update another person's financial accounts and assets, i.e. scoped delegated financial access.
## Thesis
A direct fit for Consent & Permissions, part of the orchestration spine: who can see and act for whom across a family. European-built (UK); the POA/delegation problem is universal and transfers to the US. This is connective tissue for both the upward (caregiving) and downward flows, and extends the Know-Your-Agent idea to humans acting for humans. Verify how scoped, revocable and auditable the permissions actually are.
