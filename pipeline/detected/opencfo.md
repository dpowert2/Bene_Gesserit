## One-liner
OpenCFO is building an AI-native financial operating system for mid-market CFOs, unifying accounts payable, receivable, and treasury through agentic automation with built-in human approval and audit trails.

## FinServ Signal
OpenCFO directly targets the CFO office at mid-market companies, connecting to banking infrastructure, ERP platforms, and payment rails. The platform positions itself explicitly as a "financial OS"—the connective tissue between agentic finance workflows and regulated financial plumbing. Human oversight via approvals and audit trails is built into the architecture, signalling awareness of the compliance requirements in enterprise finance.

## Team
- **Prudhvi Rao Shedimbi** and **Sankalp Singayapally** (co-founders). Engineering backgrounds at CrowdStrike, Confluent, and Bloomberg. Founded December 2025. Round led by Endiya Partners (Hyderabad-based VC), with US and India angel participation. Full platform launch expected mid-2026.

## Thesis
OpenCFO's framing as a "financial OS" is directly aligned with your core thesis: a trusted infrastructure layer that AI agents connect to, rather than competing to be the best agent itself. A mid-market CFO platform that controls banking connectivity, ERP integrations, and approval workflows is exactly the regulated infrastructure layer you want to partner with or watch closely. This gives you a potential early-stage partner for piloting AI-agent-driven treasury operations.
