## One-liner
Agentic money movement platform that autonomously collects B2B invoices, optimises payment timing, and manages cashflow — purpose-built for AI-agent-driven finance.

## FinServ Signal
Already deployed to UK SMEs and businesses, reducing late-payment rates to 9% vs a national average of ~63%. Combines proprietary payment infrastructure with AI agents that decide optimal collection actions without constant human oversight. Index Ventures led the round. Investors include Stéphane Kurgan (ex-King COO) and Andrey Khusid (Miro founder). Total raised: $30M in under two years.

## Team
Founded by Tom Pope (CEO). Previously built FinTech infrastructure products. UK-incorporated and headquartered. 

## Thesis
The B2B invoice and cashflow gap is exactly the kind of repetitive, rules-based, relationship-sensitive workflow that agentic AI can own end-to-end. Adfin's "autonomous negotiator" framing — where agents decide how and when to collect without being told — is precisely the P8 thesis: not just payment rails, but rails with embedded agent-native logic. This sits at the intersection of payment execution and the wider Agent Payment Rails category, where the agent is both the payer and the policy engine. Meaningful for your strategy as a reference point: the SMB cashflow problem mirrors the personal cashflow problem at scale, and the agentic collection architecture could serve as a template for the consumer-facing version.
