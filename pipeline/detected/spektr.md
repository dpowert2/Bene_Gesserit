# Spektr

**URL:** https://www.spektr.com
**Gap:** Compliance — KYC/KYB Automation (#6 adjacent)
**Stage:** Series A ($20M)
**Geo:** Copenhagen, Denmark (expanding to London + New York)
**Score:** 56

## One-liner
Networks of specialised AI agents that automate the manual work of KYC/KYB compliance — from onboarding through ongoing monitoring — for banks and Tier 1 financial institutions.

## FinServ Signal
Clients include Santander Leasing, Pleo, Phantom, Mercuryo, and "major U.S. marketplaces." The founders previously built HelloFlow, a digital onboarding startup sold to Trulioo (Canadian identity verification leader) in under two years for more than $50M — deeply inside the KYC ecosystem.

## Team
Repeat founders with a successful exit into KYC infrastructure (HelloFlow → Trulioo). Backed by NEA (lead, Series A), Northzone, Seedcamp, and PSV Tech. European roots with clear US expansion ambition.

## Thesis
Spektr is building agentic AI specifically for the compliance workflows banks can't avoid — KYC, KYB, ongoing monitoring. The compliance gap has shown mixed results in the pipeline (complyance 5★ for differentiated approach, bretton-ai 3★ for generic KYC/AML focus). Spektr lands squarely in the KYC/AML subfocus that has received the explicit penalty (-15 pts). The product automates what internal compliance teams do — which partially triggers the internal workflow concern — though the agents are deployed to verify external parties (customers, businesses), not to manage the institution's own processes. The distinction is real but fine. With a $20M Series A raise and Santander Leasing as a named client, it's a well-funded competitor in a contested space. The thesis wants agent-level compliance governance for external/third-party agents, not for verifying human customers. Score reflects KYC/AML penalty applied.
