## One-liner

## FinServ Signal
Founded by Szymon Pawica (ex-Goldman Sachs, observed quant fund advantage firsthand) and Mateusz Brodowicz (mathematician, previously developed quant models for a prediction markets fund). Integrating with Kalshi (regulated US prediction market), Polymarket, and Limitless. The GS provenance and prediction-markets-fund background gives this genuine quant credibility. However: substantial crypto exposure through Polymarket and EVM-based integrations.

## Team

## Thesis
This sits at the intersection of retail democratisation and algorithmic execution for prediction markets — giving you access to individual traders who can now deploy institutional-grade quant strategies without a team of engineers. The play is analogous to what this strategy is building on the equities side, applied to a newer and faster-growing market. The regulated Kalshi integration is the cleanest entry point for a regulated-market thesis. The crypto component (Polymarket, EVM) is the main friction point.

⚠️ **Novelty Gate concern:** Elastics self-describes as an "AI-native operating system for prediction markets" — the "OS" framing is a yellow flag per the current novelty filter. The actual product (plain-language trading agents for prediction markets) is genuinely novel; the "OS" is likely marketing. Score has been adjusted down slightly for this framing risk.
