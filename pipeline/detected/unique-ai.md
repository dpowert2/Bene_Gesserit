## One-liner
Unique is a vertical agentic AI platform for wealth management, asset management, and private banking — with 25+ off-the-shelf AI agents already deployed across institutions managing $2.3 trillion AUM.

## FinServ Signal
Live clients include Pictet Group (6,000 users), UBP, SIX, LGT, and Partners Group. Pictet reports efficiency gains of ~2 hours per week per employee. The platform supports back and middle-office automation via specialised agents for portfolio reporting, client onboarding, trade operations, and regulatory documentation. Total funding reaches $53M including this round.

## Team
Series A led by CommerzVentures and DN Capital, with continued participation from VI Partners and Pictet Group. Commerzbank's venture arm bringing strategic financial institution alignment. Founding team is Zurich-based with deep Swiss financial services roots.

## Thesis
Unique has a defensible moat: deployed production agents inside some of Europe's most demanding regulated wealth managers, processing live AUM at scale. This is not a pilot — it's production infrastructure for financial agents. This gives you a potential benchmark or acquisition target for the wealth management vertical of an agentic financial operating system. Their client roster ($2.3T AUM) signals that the enterprise financial institution market is ready to buy vertical agents, not build them. Watching Unique's trajectory also validates the gap you're tracking: regulated institutions want purpose-built agents, not generic LLMs bolted onto existing software.
