## One-liner
An AI-native financial home for European startup founders — automated bookkeeping, payroll, treasury, invoicing and reporting, built by ex-Stripe engineers.

## FinServ Signal
Raised by ex-Stripe (Sean Mullaney, former European CIO) with Claire Hughes Johnson (ex-COO, Stripe), Laurence Krieger (ex-UK CEO, Tide & ex-COO, Revolut), and Des Traynor (Intercom co-founder) as angels. Led by 13books Capital with Frontline Ventures and Tapestry VC returning from pre-seed. Total raised: €10M in ~18 months. The Stripe-pedigree team is credible but the product serves startup founders doing their own operational finance, not investment management.

## Team
Sean Mullaney (former European CIO at Stripe) + 20-person team heavily drawn from Stripe and Tide alumni. Very strong infrastructure pedigree for a financial operations platform.

## Thesis
Seapoint is an AI-native financial operations product for startup founders — it automates the bookkeeping, payroll, expenses, and treasury functions that a venture-backed company needs from day one. This is not investment management or wealth management; the gap fit against BG's core thesis (agents for investors) is weak. The strongest connection is S8 (Financial Planning Agent) but Seapoint serves startup founders as the end-user, not individual investors. It's closer to a B2B fintech (Pleo/Revolut Business segment) with AI automation. Logged as an infrastructure reference signal — the AI-native financial ops category is maturing rapidly, and a similar architecture applied to personal wealth planning would be a strong BG fit.
