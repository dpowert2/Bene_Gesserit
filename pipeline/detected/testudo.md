# Testudo

**URL:** https://www.testudo.co/

**Stage:** Pre-Series A (Lloyd's Lab accelerator cohort, initial commercial capacity)

## One-liner
AI liability insurance that actively monitors litigation data to underwrite and price risk from GenAI-generated outputs, covering legal costs and damages where standard commercial general liability no longer applies.

## FinServ Signal
Backed by Lloyd's Lab (Lloyd's of London's innovation accelerator) with Lloyd's market participants QBE and Atrium as capacity partners. IA Capital Group (finserv-specialist VC) as lead investor. Launched January 2026 targeting middle-to-large enterprises deploying generative AI, including financial firms facing the January 2026 exclusion of GenAI liabilities from standard CGL policies. Expanded total AI liability capacity to $9.25M in March 2026.

## Team
Proprietary "active liability monitoring" technology scanning US litigation datasets. Investors include IA Capital Group (lead), LocalGlobe, A100x, Josh Buckley, Charlie Songhurst, and Goldman Sachs partner scouts. Backed by Lloyd's Lab accelerator cohort — only selected ~12 companies per year.

## Thesis
As the agent liability gap widens — Berkshire Hathaway, Chubb, and Travelers have all sought to exclude AI-related damages from general liability policies — Testudo positions as the specialist insurer filling the void. This gives you a potential partner or investment signal in exactly the "who pays when an agent trade goes wrong?" gap the thesis identified. The active litigation monitoring moat (real-time data on AI-related lawsuits and regulatory actions) is the right shape for agent insurance — the product gets smarter the more claims data it processes. LocalGlobe backing aligns with the European/UK geography preference. Not yet explicitly focused on AI agents in financial services specifically (covers all GenAI enterprise deployments), but the Lloyd's Lab pedigree and IA Capital lead are strong finserv anchors.
