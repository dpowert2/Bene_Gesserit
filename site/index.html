Agentic Artificial Intelligence in Finance: A Comprehensive Survey

Irene Aldridge, Jolie An, Riley Burke, Michael Cao et al. · 2026-03-09

Comprehensive survey of agentic AI applications across finance, covering autonomous trading, wealth management, risk management, and compliance. Maps the landscape of how AI agents are transforming financial services from chatbot-era to fully autonomous operational agents — providing the academic foundation for the transition from passive AI to agentic AI in finance.

The Agentic Regulator: Risks for AI in Finance and a Proposed Agent-based Framework for Governance

Multiple authors · 2026-03-09

Proposes an agent-based framework for AI governance in financial services, addressing the risks of agentic AI deployment. Argues that regulators themselves should adopt agentic approaches to keep pace with AI-driven financial services — a "fight fire with fire" approach to governance. Directly validates the Compliance gap and regulatory urgency.

Agentic AI Systems Applied to Tasks in Financial Services: Modeling and Model Risk Management Crews

Multiple authors · 2026-03-09

Explores agentic crew workflows with human-in-the-loop modules for financial modeling and model risk management. The modeling crew uses specialized agents — a judge agent plus agents for EDA, feature engineering, model selection, training, evaluation, and documentation — demonstrating that multi-agent collaboration can handle complex regulated financial tasks.
